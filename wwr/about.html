History

The company was founded in 2008 by a group of adventure enthusiasts who shared a passion for exploring the untamed rivers of the region. With a vision to make whitewater rafting accessible and enjoyable for everyone, they started with just a few rafts and a lot of determination. Over the years, the company has grown significantly, expanding its fleet and team while maintaining a strong commitment to safety, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Today, we are proud to be one of the leading rafting companies, offering unforgettable adventures on some of the most beautiful rivers in the country.
